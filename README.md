## Quick Start Examples

### Example 1: Preparing for a Doctor Visit
**You:** "I have a doctor appointment tomorrow and I'm too exhausted to organize my thoughts."

**The Bridge:** Creates a concise, clinical summary you can screenshot and show your doctor.

### Example 2: Work Communication During a Flare
**You:** "I'm flaring and my boss is asking for updates but I can barely form sentences."

**The Bridge:** Drafts a professional, brief status update that explains without over-explaining.

### Example 3: Energy Management
**You:** "I have 10 things to do this weekend and no idea how to prioritize."

**The Bridge:** Breaks tasks into energy levels and helps you choose what's actually doable.

## Philosophy

**The Bridge believes:**
- Your symptoms are real, even when others can't see them
- You deserve to be taken seriously by medical professionals
- Conserving energy is smart, not lazy
- You don't owe anyone explanations about your health
- Advocacy gets easier with the right language
- Brain fog is temporary - your intelligence isn't diminished

## Who Is This For?

This skill is designed for people with:
- Chronic pain conditions (fibromyalgia, EDS, arthritis, etc.)
- Chronic fatigue (ME/CFS, long COVID, etc.)
- Autoimmune diseases (lupus, MS, rheumatoid arthritis, etc.)
- Migraine disorders
- Chronic Lyme disease
- POTS and dysautonomia
- Any "invisible" chronic illness

And for their allies who want to communicate more effectively on their behalf.
